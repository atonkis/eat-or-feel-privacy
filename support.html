Last updated: January 25, 2026

1. Getting started

Eat or Feel helps you track whether you eat from physical hunger or emotions. Before or after eating, log a short entry (about 30 seconds):

  • Choose whether the eating was self-initiated or triggered by an external circumstance
  • Rate your hunger level from 0 to 10
  • If hunger was low, select the emotion you felt
  • Record whether you ate or resisted

Over time, the app shows statistics and insights so you can see patterns (e.g. emotional vs physical eating, resistance rate, risk times).

2. Statistics and views

In the Statistics section you can switch between:

  • Day – view and pick a single day
  • Week – view a week; use the horizontal slider to pick the week
  • Month – view a month; use the slider to pick the month

Long-press the period selector (Day / Week / Month) to open the date picker. Charts and session list update according to the selected period.

3. PDF reports

You can generate and share PDF reports from the Statistics screen:

  • Use the share/export option to create a PDF
  • The report includes sessions, statistics summary, and charts
  • PDFs are created on your device; you choose where to save or share them (e.g. with a therapist or for your own records)

4. Subscription and premium

Premium features (e.g. full statistics, PDF reports) may require a subscription. To manage your subscription:

  • Open Settings in the app
  • Tap Manage subscription
  • Follow the prompts to view, change, or cancel your subscription (through the App Store or Google Play, depending on your device)

5. Language

Eat or Feel is available in multiple languages. To change the app language:

  • Open Settings
  • Tap Language and select your preferred language

6. Data and privacy

All your data is stored only on your device. We do not collect or transmit your entries to our servers. For details, see our Privacy Policy. To remove all data, you can uninstall the app.
