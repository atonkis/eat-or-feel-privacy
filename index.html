Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 25, 2026

1. Information Collection

Eat or Feel collects the following information:

  • Eating session records (date, time, hunger level)
  • Emotional states
  • Eating reasons

All data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Generating statistics and charts
  • Creating PDF reports
  • Personal progress tracking

Data is never shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • SharedPreferences (app settings)
  • Local SQLite database (records)

Data is never sent to external servers.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export your data (PDF)
  • Delete all data by uninstalling the app
  • Cancel subscriptions at any time

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact:

Email: info@gki.lt
Website: https://www.gki.lt

Eat or Feel is a self-reflection and habit-awareness tool. The app does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
